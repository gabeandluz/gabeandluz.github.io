Our wedding will take place at Camp Hoffman in South Kingstown, Rhode Island. It's a gorgeous girl scout camp about an hour outside Providence. We'll kick things off with our ceremony under a charming old maple tree. Afterwards we'll party it up in their rustic dining hall.

It wouldn't be a Gabe & Luz party without seriously good eats. Our cocktail hour and reception will feature an array of barbecue favorites, hot off the grill. Just wait until you see the dessert spread Luz has selected.

We'll cap off the night with a roaring bonfire. S'mores anyone?
We ask that cocktail attire be followed for the ceremony and reception, but bring something comfortable if you plan on staying overnight.

We've arranged sleeping accommodations at the camp for everyone who wants to stay. You'll have the choice of private cabins — perfect for families — and bunk rooms which can sleep up to a dozen people. For those who'd prefer to spread out, Camp Hoffman has plenty of space to bring your own tent or camper.

If the rustic life isn't for you, there's a number of hotels and bed and breakfasts in the area.
  • Jujubes Inn & Suites Monroe
  • Cupcake Lodge Woodinville
